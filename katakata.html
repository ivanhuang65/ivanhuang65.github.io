about

katakata publishes fiction, poetry, and essays in english and bahasa malaysia.

we read both languages without preference. we want work that is precise and grounded.

based in kuala lumpur.

submit

send fiction, poetry, and essays in english or bahasa malaysia. simultaneous submissions are fine; let us know if the work is accepted elsewhere.

fiction and essays: up to 3,500 words. poetry: up to five poems. reply within eight weeks.

submit@katakata.my
